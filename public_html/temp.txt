 
	
<h2 style="color: #000000;">Industry Speaker event - Continuously Delivering Value At Zendesk</h2>
<p>Presenter:&nbsp;<strong>Adel Smee - Team Lead at Zendesk</strong></p>
<p><em>"An Agile software development team needs to be good at one thing - responding to change. The mechanism of change is the software delivery system. By considering the technical, workflow and people elements of continuous delivery at the beginning of a project, rather than as an afterthought, you can ensure earlier competitive advantage.</em></p>
<p><em>At Zendesk we&rsquo;ve built a continuous delivery pipeline to ensure we maximise our ability to deliver software of value at speed. This talk includes an overview of the technologies we use to build that pipeline, how we run it at web scale and why we chose this particular process. I&rsquo;ll also explain how we create an environment that fosters the intrinsic elements crucial for the success of this kind of software delivery: trust and collaboration</em>"<em>.</em></p>
<p><strong><strong>Confirmed date</strong>:&nbsp;</strong>Thursday, 31st March 2016 (11.00am)<br /><strong>Location:&nbsp;</strong>ATC205, Level 2 Advanced Technologies Centre, Swinburne University, Hawthorn Campus</p>
